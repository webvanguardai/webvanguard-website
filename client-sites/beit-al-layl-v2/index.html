Our Story

"Beit Al Layl started as a family argument about whose grandmother made the better hummus. It ended with a restaurant."

The recipes are hers. The space is ours. Every dish on this menu was tested at a kitchen table in Beirut before it was ever served in Dubai. We haven't changed them. We never will.
