# Brand Checklist - WebVanguard

## Tone of Voice
- **Calm but sharp** (tranquilo pero afilado)
- **Creative, honest, no bullshit** (creativo, honesto, sin relleno)
- **Dry humor when appropriate** (humor seco cuando encaje)
- **Premium, not pretentious** (de lujo, sin ser pretencioso)

## Do Say (Palabras clave)
- precision, excellence, authority, convert, elevate, disciplined, premium, sophisticated, clear, fast, credibility.
- "Websites that convert."
- "Digital authority."
- "Less agency theatre. More commercial clarity."

## Don’t Say (Palabras prohibidas/evitar)
- "cheap", "amazing", "unbelievable", "revolutionary" (salvo contexto muy específico).
- Términos hype sin sustento ("AI-powered", "blockchain", "metaverse") a menos que el proyecto lo requiera.
- "We are the best" → en su lugar: "We deliver discipline."
- "No problem" → "My pleasure" o "Certainly."

## CTAs (Call-to-Action)
- **Primary:** "Start your project", "Let’s talk.", "Get your audit"
- **Secondary:** "View work", "Download brief", "Book a call"
- **Button style:** Accent color (#1e3a5f), hover gold (#c5a059), arrow icon on primary.

## Visual Identity
- Font: Inter (sans) for body, Clash Display (or similar) for headings.
- Colors: #0a0a0a (bg), #1e3a5f (accent), #c5a059 (hover), #2a2a2a (section numbers), white/gray-400 (text).
- Imagery: high-quality, minimal retouching,的真实场景, no stock fake.
- Layout: negative space, asymmetric grids, thin borders (1px).

## Messaging Rules
- No exclamation marks más de 1 por párrafo.
- Sentences: max 20 palabras.
- Headlines: 3-5 palabras首选.
- Avoid passive voice.

## Social Media (YouTube, LinkedIn)
- Same voice, shorter format.
- Thumbnails: dark background, bold white text <5 words, single accent color.
- Video hooks: first 5 seconds must state viewer benefit.

## Compliance
- No overpromise ("guaranteed #1 ranking" → avoid).
- Respect GDPR/UA regulations; no spam.

---

Test: every piece of copy should feel like it’s written by a serious studio that gets results, not a hype machine.
